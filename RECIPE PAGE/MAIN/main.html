CINCO DE MAYO RECIPES

Cinco de Mayo, or the Fifth of May, commemorates the Mexican army's victory over the French Empire at the Battle of Puebla on May 5, 1862. This historic battle symbolizes Mexican resilience and unity in the face of adversity. In the United States, Cinco de Mayo has evolved into a celebration of Mexican culture and heritage, featuring festivities that include parades, music, dancing, traditional foods, and vibrant decorations. It's a time to honor Mexican contributions and share in the pride of a rich cultural legacy. On this website, you can find several recipes to prepare for this celebration, such as:

  • Beer Margarita
  • Enchiladas Verdes
  • Pico De Gallo De Alicia
